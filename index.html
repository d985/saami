services

You are unique–so your therapy and treatment plan should be, too. At saami speech therapy, we have experience in a variety of settings (acute care, acute rehabilitation, outpatient, preschool to high school, early intervention) that position us to work well with children and adult clients.

We believe in a relationship-based approach that encompasses:

  • Holistic, whole-person approach
  • Evidence-based practice
  • Functional, meaningful, tailored-to-you goals

If you are curious about whether or not speech therapy is right for you or a loved one, please reach out. Schedule a call here, we’re happy to talk.

Adult Speech Therapy +
We utilize evidence-based practice to establish and achieve functional and meaningful goals for our clients in the following areas:
  • Cognitive-linguistic skills (attention, memory, executive functioning)
  • Speech (dysarthria, acquired apraxia of speech)
  • Expressive and receptive language (aphasia)
  • Swallowing
  • AAC
  • Voice
We are proud to be SPEAK OUT!® Therapy providers for clients with Parkinson’s.
Pediatric Speech Therapy +
We love working with our kids and specialize in the following areas:
  • Speech delays (Expressive/receptive language)
  • Articulation and speech sound disorders
  • Early intervention/parent coaching

about

Leslie Chen, M.A., CCC-SLP

Hi! I’m Leslie and I’ve had experience in a variety of settings (acute rehabilitation, neuro outpatient, pediatric outpatient, acute care/inpatient, preschool and elementary schools). My favorite activity is hearing from clients about what they would like to get back to (e.g., playing mahjong again, saying “I love you” to their partner, or simply being loud enough to order their Starbucks again) and tailoring an evidence-based treatment plan to achieve their goals.

Outside of the speech therapy realm, I really enjoy sitting in my garden with my husband, baby, and dog!

Certifications:

  • SPEAK OUT!® Provider
  • Hanen-certified SLP
  • McNeil Dysphagia Therapy Provider (MDTP)
  • ASHA CCC-SLP
